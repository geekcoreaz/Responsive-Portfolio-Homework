About Me

I was born in Los Angeles and spent the first 17 years of my life there. I moved to Tucson in 1994 and completed my senior year of high school in 1995. For roughly the past 20 years I have worked in IT both for companies like AOL and Square Enix as well as being self employed. In April 2017, I started working a vape shop in Tucson and I can honestly say I'm the happiest working there, although I still continue to do IT work on the side. I want to continue to develop skills with Full Stack Development and I plan on making it a career in a couple of years.

My main hobbies include playing games and listening to music. I particularily like playing games on PC, though I do have both a PlayStation 4 and and an XBox One. I also enjoy playing Dungeons and Dragons. My latest hobby addiction has been buying bluetooth speakers and headphones/earbuds. I've purchased 8 of them in the past few months.
